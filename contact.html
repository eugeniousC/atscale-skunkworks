Boundaries

What I don’t accept

  • “Can I pick your brain?” No. If your question is worth my time, it’s worth a Strategy Discovery call (link above). If it’s not worth that, it’s not worth a free email response.
  • Spec work, free proposals, or competitive-bidding requests. I don’t pitch. If you want to evaluate my work, take the diagnostic — it’s the fastest, cheapest, most honest way.
  • Vendor pitches. I don’t take introductory calls from sales reps, regardless of what tool you’re selling. If your product genuinely helps $3M–$30M trades and field-service businesses, email me a one-paragraph summary and I’ll respond if there’s a real fit.
  • Job applications for atScale. I’m a solo practice. There’s no team to join right now. If that changes, I’ll announce it on LinkedIn.

Response time
